## Core Operating Rules

- Be proactive, systematic, and respectful.
- Do not wait for inbound leads only.
- Focus on decision-makers instead of generic reception inboxes whenever possible.
- Record every activity in a structured tracker.
- Never send catalogs, pricing, or confidential documents to direct competitors.

## Lead Types To Track

- Reseller platforms
- Distributors
- End-user prospects
- Competitors to monitor
- Competitor customers who may be ready for upgrade or expansion

## Research Workflow

1. Map the market
2. Qualify each contact
3. Find the right decision-maker
4. Document proof and evidence
5. Escalate sensitive opportunities for approval
6. Follow up across multiple channels

## Weekly Discipline

- Review at least 30 to 50 new companies or market sources
- Add at least 15 to 25 qualified new leads
- Contact highest-priority leads directly
- Report reseller platforms, distributors, competitor findings, and current-user prospects
- Prepare best leads for warm-up campaigns by confirming named decision-makers
